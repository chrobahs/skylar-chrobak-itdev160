Best Disney Product Database!

All the bags each have pros and cons, I'd like to go through them all!

#1 Bag Types: Backpacks

Vera Bradley Campus Backpack Vera Bradley Mini Backpack

Backpacks are amazing for when you are with your family, or you are not staying on resort where you can just have your things sent back to your room. Water, pin lanyards, Mouse-ear headbands and leftovers fit while still being comfortable to carry. Also buy one with the pockets for water bottles/cups in case you don't want to carry them.

#2 Bag Types: Large Crossbody

Vera Bradley Large Hipster

Large crossbodies are good for a wallet and a nice camera to have the photographers use, and a place to put your ears if you don't want to wear them all day, which I get because mine end up giving headaches still.

#3 Bag Types: Small/Mini Crossbody

Vera Bradley Mini Hipster

This bag is the best bag if someone only needs to carry a small wallet and your phone. It is compact but helpful as there is no extra storing on rides, like tucking it underneath the seat ahead of you.

Which ever bag you choose, the most important part is that you are comfortable, as it will be what you wear all day, and in your pictures!
